Saco Lake
Bath House

type: architecture
year: 2017

Situated in Crawford Notch State Park, overlooking Saco Lake, this small scale bath house is meant for hikers to rest along as they travel along the trail in the park. A design exploration executed through experiential collages and models, focusing on the materials of wood and concrete. A bathhouse for a curated experience from the ground to the sky.
