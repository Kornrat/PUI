Data Driven Display

type: UX / UI
year: 2018
with: Sean Park, Rebecca Zheng

Large environmental displays engage member of the community and must be highly readable and easily understood. These displays must organize the data into valuable meaning to the user. Skillfully using typography, composition, and color are crucial in creating an effective design that meets the needs of stakeholders and users.
